01
Eat Every Day. No Exceptions.
Your body is building something. It cannot build from nothing. Every training day demands fuel. This is non-negotiable.
02
Your Daily Foundation
Morning — Weetabix with milk. Add a banana or apple.
Midday — Subway footlong, all veg. Keep it.
Evening — Rice with yoghurt. Add nuts or seeds where possible.
03
More. Not Different.
Don't overhaul what you eat. Eat more of it. Bigger portions. A snack between meals. Build the habit of feeding yourself first.
04
Water First
Minimum 2 litres daily. Performance, joints, skin, focus — all of it runs on water. Carry a bottle everywhere.
05
Get Proper Support
For a full plan built around your training — speak to your GP. Free NHS dietitian referral. One appointment. Worth every minute.
